My Three Dogs: Agnes, Eddie, and Weasy

I have three charming canines, who I walk each day.

  1. Agnes is a great pyrenees.
  2. Eddie is a labrador.
  3. Weasy is a terrier.
Benefits of walking dogs:
  • Great for their health
  • Better behavior when they have something to look forward to
  • Sense of purpose

To spend your life chasing a roaming dog, check out the National Great Pyrenees Rescue!
